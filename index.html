We do not control this data collection. Please review third-party ad policies for more information. Data Security We take reasonable measures to protect data but cannot guarantee 100% security. You use AFWeather at your own risk. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately. Your Rights Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights. Changes We may update this policy.
